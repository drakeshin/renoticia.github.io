Here's your look at the BrokenLore: ASCEND announcement trailer for the upcoming first-person psychological horror game about obsession, fame, and fear. Join Ren and Yui, two renowned rooftop climbers, as they attempt a forbidden nighttime ascent of Japan’s tallest building, the mysterious Elysium Tower.What begins as the ultimate stunt for their channel, SKYLIMIT, soon spirals into a nightmare. A supernatural entity, the Rokurokubi, emerges to haunt them, a twisted reflection of their deepest terror: becoming forgotten and irrelevant.The higher they climb, the more reality unravels. For Ren and Yui, stopping means losing everything, but reaching the top might cost them their sanity or their lives.BrokenLore: ASCEND will be available on PC, PlayStation 5 (PS5), and Xbox Series X/S.
