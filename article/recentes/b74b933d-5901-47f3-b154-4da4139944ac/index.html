HBO has renewed its Game of Thrones spinoff shows for more seasons

Publicado em 20/11/2025 às 16:40

Ver Fonte Original

HBO's latest Game of Thrones prequel series hasn't even premiered yet, but there are already plans for it to run for at least one more season.

Today, HBO announced both A Knight for the Seven Kingdoms and House of the Dragon have been renewed for second and fourth seasons, respectively. In addition to the Game of Thrones prequels, the network has also put an order in for more episodes of Tim Robinson's The Chair Company, Rachel Sennott's I Love LA, and Task starring Mark Ruffalo.
