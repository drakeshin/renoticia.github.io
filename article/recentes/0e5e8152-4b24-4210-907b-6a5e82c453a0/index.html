About

Master and the maids are stuck cleaning up after Master’s uncle yet again, this time with an old apartment complex that he owns.
After trying to come up with a way to use the building, they decide to renovate it into the “Empire Manor!”
This time, in addition to Muku, Majime, and Rindere, the following 8 maids will join in:
Bookworm, Sweet Little Devil, Ladylike, Secretary Maid, Soft Cuddly Girl, Blunt Girl, Preppy Girl, and even Gyaru!
You’ll get two new modes to play, Upgrade Mode and Business Mode, and there are Empire Manor backgrounds showing the property both before and after renovation.
This huge expansion pack also includes 12 events and 22 yotogi for you to enjoy!
On top of that, it comes bundled with the Girly Active Set, sure to be beloved by energetic girls.

Key Features

  1. New Modes
    In Upgrade Mode, you’ll work with your maids to repair the run-down Empire Manor. You’ll be able to enjoy scenes of the maids working together, as well as some slightly smutty scenes with Master.
    Once you’ve finished upgrades, you’ll be able to play Business Mode and invite guests to the Empire Manor for a new kind of guest service.
  1. Additional Yotogi Skills
    New Yotogi for Upgrade Mode and Business Mode will be added for all 11 personalities. You’ll also be able to play the 22 yotogi skills included in Memories Mode.

Please Read Before Purchase
・ You will need the COM3D2 base game to play this DLC.
To play this DLC’s content with Bookworm, Sweet Little Devil, Ladylike, Blunt Girl, Secretary Maid, Soft Cuddly Girl, Preppy Girl, or Gyaru, you will need to have installed the respective personality packs and hired maids of those personalities.
