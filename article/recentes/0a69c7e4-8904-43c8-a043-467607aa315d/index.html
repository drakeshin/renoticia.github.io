What immediately stood out was the pills littering the globe, surrounded by syringes jutting toward the sky like weapons. Others noticed the war images, such as a cargo ship being shot with an arrow.

“Would I launch strikes on Mexico to stop drugs? It’s OK with me. Whatever we have to do to stop drugs. Mexico is…look I looked at Mexico City over the weekend. There’s some big problems over there.
